About Me

Bridging the gap between pharmaceutical science and modern technology to build data-driven healthcare solutions.

I am a Pharma Technologist currently contributing to research and technological innovation at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh. With roots in both pharmaceutical sciences and computer engineering, I explore how machine learning, data analytics, and software architecture can improve healthcare delivery and research.

My work spans pharmacological quality systems, analytical method development, and building intelligent tools — from Chronic Kidney Disease detection using ML to 3D simulation systems and e-commerce platforms. I believe that the health sector deserves the same engineering rigor as top-tier tech.
